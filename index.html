Sailing to Allyship

Please read the information below before connecting with your Sailing to Allyship partner.


Description

Welcome to the Sailing to Allyship program! In a few moments you will be connected via our video conferencing platform to a White partner who is interested in allyship. Before you get started there are a few steps you should take to set up. We've outlined these below.

Instructions

Before you connect, you will be asked to turn on your webcam and microphone. This is so you can have a face-to-face conversation with your Sailing to Allyship partner.

When you are done reading Alex's responses please press the "Enable Video/Audio" button to set up your camera and microphone.

Your browser will ask for permission to access your camera and then your microphone. If you would only like to connect with audio, deny access to your camera and accept access to your microphone.

When you are set up and ready to meet Alex, please click the "Connect" button to continue.
If you do not see the "Connect" button, make sure to click "Enable Video/Audio" and set up your camera and microphone first.
